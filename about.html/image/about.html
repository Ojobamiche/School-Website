OUR ADMISSION PROCESS

HOW TO APPLY

Our Admissions Philosophy We believe in fostering a diverse and inclusive learning community that reflects the richness of the world we live in.
Our admissions process is designed to identify students who are not only academically capable but also share our passion for curiosity, creativity, and character development.

The Admissions Process
Our admissions process is designed to be transparent, straightforward, and accessible to all prospective families. Here are the key steps:

Application: Begin by submitting an online application form.
Please provide accurate and complete information to help us understand your child better.

Assessment: We conduct age-appropriate assessments to evaluate your child's readiness for the chosen program. These assessments are designed to be stress-free and child-friendly.

Enrollment: Once accepted, you can complete the enrollment process, including submitting the required documents and fees.
